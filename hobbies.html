As I mentioned on the homepage, I have a number of nerdy hobbies. Nothing too crazy, but may as well mention them.

I've watched anime since I was a kid. These days I'll often turn on a dubbed anime while multitasking, if the other task doesn't require too much focus.

An extension of watching anime, was reading manga and light novels. Once I catch up to an anime, if I wanted to keep going on the story, the next step would be to read the previous source.

Another one of my nerdy hobbies is board games. My car trunk is filled to the brim with board games, some casual, some more intensive, always with me if I find a context to pull em' out.

Lastly, video games. In recent years I've humoured climbing ranks, and self imposed challenges, helps keep things fresh. Often this is done with friends or watching videos (including anime).
